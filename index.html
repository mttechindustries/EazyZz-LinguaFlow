Core Scientific Principles

Targeted Memory Reactivation (TMR)

Cues are delivered during NREM Stage 3 sleep, precisely timed with slow-wave oscillations to reinforce memory traces. (Ref: Hu et al., 2020, *Nature Neuroscience*).

Theta-Gamma Neural Entrainment

40Hz gamma bursts are phase-locked to endogenous theta waves (4-8Hz) to promote synaptic plasticity and information binding. (Ref: Iaccarino et al., 2016, *Nature* on 40Hz gamma).
